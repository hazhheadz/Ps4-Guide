    Required Reading

    Select the appropriate page for your version from the chart below. Note that the “from” and “to” fields are inclusive. This means that, for example, the “from 3.50 to 4.50” row includes 3.55, 4.00, 4.06, 4.07, and 4.50. (Ps4 updates are spotty, so not all updates inbetween are included).

    For all versions you can also USB Update (or use a standard system update, not recommended) to get to a higher version to follow its instructions.

    Your device version can be found at the middle of the System Information.
